# Initial vs Final

Stage: final comparison.
Consumed: `01-initial/screenshot.png` and `10-final/final-screenshot.png`.

## Verdict

Improved.

## What Improved

- The page now has one primary object: the daily speaking task.
- The CTA is singular.
- The side dashboard and future recap preview are gone.
- The skill snapshot is secondary and compact.
- The headline moved from generic landing-page copy to task-specific product copy.
- The page no longer asks the user to understand the whole system before starting.

## What Was Rejected

- generated feedback previews
- future locked steps
- coach-prep panels
- extra progress links
- dashboard stats on the daily start page
- process commentary visible in the product UI

## Remaining Weaknesses

- This is still a static demonstration, not a production app.
- Real recording controls and interaction states are not modeled.
- The final visual style is serviceable, not final brand work.

## Ship Status

Good enough as a public process demo. Continue iteration only if the demo needs production-grade
visual polish rather than workflow proof.
