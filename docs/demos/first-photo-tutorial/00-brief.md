# Brief: First Photo Tutorial

## Product

A photo-coaching app that teaches better phone photography through one guided shot at a time.

## User

First-time user, opened the app after installing it, phone in hand, willing to take one photo.

## Flow goal

Teach three tools through action:

- light
- angle
- timing

The user should finish with a visibly improved photo and one clear next action.

## Constraints

- Start in the camera, not a welcome page.
- Use one instruction at a time.
- Use the user's own before/after as the payoff.
- Do not show fake progress stats during first-run tutorial.

## Non-goals

- Account setup.
- Gallery management.
- Full photography theory.
- Social sharing.
- Analytics dashboards.
